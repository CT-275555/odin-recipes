Tri-tip Roast

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Tri-tip Roast
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Mix the spices together in a 1:1:1 ratio by weight
  3. Use 2Tbsp of the mixture to cover the roast with and let sit for half an hour
  4. Cook for 30 minutes then check the internal temp
  5. Continue to cook checking the temp every 5 minutes until the internal tempeture is 130°F
  6. Let the roast rest befor slicing

Slicing Tips

The grain of a Tri-tip runs in 2 directions similarly to brisket always make sure to cut accross the grain and not with it.
